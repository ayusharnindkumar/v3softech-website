About V3 Software Technology

V3 Software Technology is a leading CAD design service provider based in Pune, delivering precision engineering solutions to industries across automotive, manufacturing, and fabrication sectors. Our experienced design team specializes in Press Tool Design, Welding Fixture Design, Special Purpose Machines, Tubing Fixtures, Fabrication & Structure Design, 2D Drafting, and 3D Modelling using industry-standard tools like SolidWorks, CATIA, Creo, NX, and AutoCAD.

We work closely with clients to understand their production requirements and deliver accurate, inspection-ready designs within timelines. Whether you need a one-time design project or an ongoing design partnership, V3 is your trusted engineering design partner.

V3 Software Technology is a premier CAD training and design consultancy based in Pune. We empower students and professionals with in-demand design skills across Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, and IT domains.

Our expert trainers bring real-world industry experience into the classroom, ensuring students are job-ready from day one. We combine hands-on project training with placement support to give every student a competitive edge.

We also offer corporate design and training services — helping businesses upskill their teams and outsource specialized CAD design work efficiently.
